# Hardware Budget Calculator

The Budget Calculator helps students and educators plan their hardware investments across different tiers and configurations. Use this tool to compare on-premise setups with cloud alternatives and optimize your spending based on your specific needs.

<BudgetCalculator />

## How to Use the Calculator

1. **Select Your Hardware Tier**: Choose between Edge Kit, Proxy Robot, or Premium Lab based on your requirements and budget.

2. **Configure Options**.
   - Include maintenance costs for long-term planning
   - Add training expenses for skill development
   - Factor in software licenses

3. **Add Cloud Alternatives**: Compare with cloud instances for flexible computing resources.

4. **Review Projections**: See monthly, annual, and 3-year cost projections.

5. **Optimize**: Get personalized recommendations for cost savings and performance improvements.

6. **Export**: Download your configuration as CSV or JSON for documentation and sharing.

## Hardware Tiers Overview

### Edge Kit (~$700)
- **Ideal for**: Beginners, students, edge AI projects
- **Includes**: Jetson Orin Nano, Intel RealSense D435i
- **Use cases**: Learning basic AI concepts, edge deployment, small-scale projects

### Proxy Robot ($1,800-$3,000)
- **Ideal for**: Advanced students, research, prototype development
- **Includes**: RTX GPU, Raspberry Pi, robot arm, sensors
- **Use cases**: Advanced AI development, robotics research, prototyping

### Premium Lab ($16,000+)
- **Ideal for**: Professional development, advanced research, production systems
- **Includes**: High-end workstation, advanced sensors, professional robotics
- **Use cases**: Industrial applications, advanced research, production systems

## Cost Optimization Tips

- **Start Small**: Begin with Edge Kit and upgrade as needed
- **Hybrid Approach**: Combine on-premise hardware with cloud resources
- **Educational Discounts**: Look for academic pricing on hardware and software
- **Shared Resources**: Consider shared lab setups for educational institutions
